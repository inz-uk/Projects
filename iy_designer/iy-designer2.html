When my client approaches me for a quote, I will book them for a free consultation. After the meeting if the client is happy I will then go ahead and prepare a quotation for the services they would like.

I like to give my clients a breakdown of the quote as this allows the client to understand what they are being charged for. I work in four stages for my projects as this is better for myself and a lot easier for my clients.

The four phases consist of:

Discovery Phase - Initial phone calls, meetings, consultations, research, planning, scheduling, and develop site structure/sitemap.
Creative Phase - User experience designs, wireframing and web design mock ups.
Development Phase - Convert designs and browser testing.
Launch Phase - Review, Q&A, moving website to live, client training and final payment (once the client is pleased with everything).
